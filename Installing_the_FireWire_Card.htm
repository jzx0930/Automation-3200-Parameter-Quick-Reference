Installing the FireWire or HyperWire™ Card

If you want to operate without a FireWire or HyperWire card, refer to the Operation without a FireWire or HyperWire Card section of FireWire or HyperWire™ Card Options to configure your system. To install a FireWire or HyperWire card, use the procedure that follows.

Danger: Before you install the FireWire or HyperWire card, power off the computer.

To Install the FireWire Card

NOTE: Do not install the FireWire card in a PCI Express or PCI expansion chassis. You must install the card in a PCI Express or PCI slot, Í4 or larger, on the motherboard.
  1. Power off the computer and disconnect the computer from the power supply.
  2. To get access to the motherboard, remove the computer system cover.
  3. Find an available PCI Express or PCI slot. If necessary, refer to your computer manual for information about available slots.
    4. Available PCI Express or PCI Slot

  4. Remove the cover of the available slot that you want to use.
  5. Carefully push the FireWire card into the slot.
    6. FireWire Card Installed

  6. Attach the metal tab on the card to your computer with the method in your computer manual. This is typically a mounting screw.
  7. Put the cover on the computer.
  8. Connect the computer to the power supply and power on the computer.

The driver for the HyperWire™ card is installed with the A3200 software. You can install the software before or after you install the HyperWire card.

To Install the HyperWire™ Card

NOTE: Do not install the HyperWire card in a PCI Express expansion chassis. You must install the card in a PCI Express slot, Í4 or larger, on the motherboard.
  1. Power off the computer and disconnect the computer from the power supply.
  2. To get access to the motherboard, remove the computer system cover.
  3. Find an available PCI Express slot, Í4 or larger. If necessary, refer to your computer manual for information about available slots.
    4. Available PCI Express Slot

  4. Remove the cover of the available slot that you want to use.
  5. Carefully push the HyperWire card into the slot.
    6. HyperWire Card Installed

  6. Attach the metal tab on the card to your computer with the method in your computer manual. This is typically a mounting screw.
  7. Put the cover on the computer.
  8. Connect the computer to the power supply and power on the computer.
