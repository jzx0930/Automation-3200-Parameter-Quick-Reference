Closed-Loop Models (stages with feedback only)

If the direction of travel is aligned with gravity and the payload causes a deflection that is greater than three percent of the closed-loop travel, Aerotech recommends that you do these steps.

  1. Mount and attach the payload to the piezo stage.
  2. Use the AbsoluteFeedbackOffset Calculator in Configuration Manager. For information about how to use this calculator, refer to Using the AbsoluteFeedbackOffset Calculator.

To calculate the deflection that is caused by the payload, you must know the stiffness in the direction of motion of the piezo stage. Refer to the Basic Specifications topic in the hardware manual for your piezo stage.

When you use the AbsoluteFeedbackOffset Calculator, the controller maximizes the closed-loop travel of the piezo stage by executing a routine that applies a feedback offset in the software.

For Example

Your system uses a QNP-60-500L-C stage in which the direction of motion is aligned with gravity. The stage has these specifications.

Table:     Basic Specifications

Specification Value

Closed-Loop Travel

500 μm

Stiffness (in Direction of Motion)

0.27 N/μm

Payload

475 g

The calculated deflection of the stage is 17.3 μm.

Deflection = Force/Stiffness

Because 17.3 μm is greater than three percent of the closed-loop travel of the stage, you must use the AbsoluteFeedbackOffset Calculator.
