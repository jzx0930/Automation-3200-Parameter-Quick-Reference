Identifying Errors in A3200 Applications

The following sections show you how the A3200 Firmware Loader application identifies errors that have related Help topics and how other A3200 applications identify errors with a status bar.

Firmware Loader

In Firmware Loader, errors are shown in the Operation Log. In the Help column of the Operation Log, click the Help button to open the Help topic for the specific error that is shown.

Figure: Firmware Loader Operation Log

Other A3200 Applications

In Configuration Manager, Digital Scope, and Motion Composer, errors are shown in a status bar at the bottom of the screen. Click the Help button that is shown to the right of the status bar to open the Help topic for the specific error that is shown.
