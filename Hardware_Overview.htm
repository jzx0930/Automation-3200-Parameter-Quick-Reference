Hardware Overview

The A3200 product family includes FireWire and HyperWire drives. While many features are common to all drive types, some drives have unique features. Also, some features are standard and some features are available only if you purchase an optional I/O board or other option.

The tables that follow show the availability of different features for each of the supported drive types.

Table Legend

Standard Equipment
 

NA

Not available on that model

Requires the purchase of additional equipment
