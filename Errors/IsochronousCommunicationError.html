IsochronousCommunicationError

An error occurred with isochronous communication: <Location>

Table:     Location Codes

Location Code Interpretation

1

A timeout occurred during the initial isochronous transmit event.

2

A timeout occurred during the initial isochronous receive event.

3

A timeout occurred while waiting for an isochronous receive event from all drives.

An error occurred during isochronous communications.

Make sure that all drives are powered up and that all drives have a unique communication channel (assigned by the drive's device number). Refer to the Communication Channel Settings section of the hardware manual for your drive for more information about setting a unique communication channel.

The sections that follow tell you what might cause the error and give you solutions.

A timeout occurred during the initial isochronous transmit event.

Cause

The FireWire or HyperWire card is not configured to work with the A3200.

Solution

Configure the FireWire or HyperWire card to work with A3200. Refer to Configuring and Verifying the FireWire or HyperWire™ Card for more information.

 

Cause

The FireWire or HyperWire card is defective.

Solution

Replace the FireWire or HyperWire card.

 

Cause

You are using an unusual PC and have no devices connected to the FireWire or HyperWire card. If no devices are connected to the FireWire or HyperWire card, some laptops or industrial PCs automatically shut down FireWire or HyperWire transmission and cause this error.

Solution

Connect a drive to the FireWire or HyperWire card and try again. If you are using FireWire, you can also connect a FireWire repeater to the card and try again. If you want to run without any drives connected to the computer, in the Connection Settings dialog, select Operate without a card . Refer to FireWire or HyperWire™ Card Options for more information.

A timeout occurred during the initial isochronous receive event.

Cause

The drives are off.

Solution

Verify the power connections to all drives on the FireWire or HyperWire chain. And verify that all drives are powered.

 

Cause

The FireWire or HyperWire cable is defective.

Solution

Verify that your FireWire or HyperWire cables are working correctly. Test each cable separately. If necessary, replace the FireWire or HyperWire cable.

 

Cause

The FireWire or HyperWire card is defective.

Solution

FireWire: If you are using FireWire, try connecting the FireWire cable to a different FireWire connector on the FireWire card (most cards have three FireWire connectors) and on the drive (all drives have at least two, and possibly three, connectors). If the problem still occurs, replace the FireWire card.

HyperWire: If you are using HyperWire, replace the HyperWire card.

 

Cause

A drive is defective.

Solution

Connect only one drive to the PC at a time to find the drive that failed.

A timeout occurred while waiting for an isochronous receive event from all drives.

Cause

Two drives incorrectly have the same communication channel settings.

Solution

Because all drives must have unique communication channel settings, you must change one of them. To find the duplicate settings, open the Status Utility (click OK for any warning messages that appear). Click the Drive Nodes tab and look at the Starting Axis row.

If you cannot find the cause of this error, or if you tried the recommended solution and receive the error again, record the text of the error message and contact Aerotech Global Technical Support.
