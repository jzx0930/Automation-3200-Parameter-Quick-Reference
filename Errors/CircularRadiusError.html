CircularRadiusError

A circular radius error occurred: <Location>

Table:     Location Codes

Location Code Interpretation

1

A circular radius of zero was specified.

2

The circular radius is too short.

3

The center point is incorrect.

The sections that follow tell you what might cause the error and give you solutions.

A circular radius of zero was specified.

Cause

You specified a circular arc that has a radius of zero.

Solution

Refer to CW or CCW commands to correct your program.

The circular radius is too short.

An arc (CW or CCW) was specified with a radius too short to reach the target position from the start position.

Causes

  1. The specified radius is too small.
  2. The starting or ending point is incorrect.

Solutions

  1. Correct the radius.
  2. Correct the starting or ending point.
The center point is incorrect.

Cause

Circular radius errors can occur if the center point of an arc (CW or CCW commands) is specified by the IJK method. Errors occur when the distance between the center point and starting point of an arc is different than the distance between the center point and end point of the arc.

Solution

Make sure that the CW or CCW arguments are correct in your program. If these arguments are correct, try the following procedures.

  • Use the R method instead of the IJK method for specifying the circle. In this method, you specify the target point of the arc and the radius of the arc (for example, CW R5 X10 instead of CW I5 X10). The R method prevents circular radius errors because you do not over-specify the problem.
  • Configure your CAD post-processor to produce higher precision in its outputs. This applies to the I, J, and K, and to the X and Y coordinates.
  • Increase the RadiusErrorThreshold parameter until the controller does not generate an error.
  • Set the RadiusErrorThreshold parameter to 0 to prevent all radius errors. This can be dangerous because errors in the CAD post-processor are not detected.

If you cannot find the cause of this error, or if you tried the recommended solution and receive the error again, record the text of the error message and contact Aerotech Global Technical Support.
