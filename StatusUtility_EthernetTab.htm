Ethernet Tab of the Status Utility

The Ethernet tab of the Status Utility shows diagnostic information that is useful for debugging Ethernet connections to the controller.

Figure: Ethernet Tab in the Status Utility

PC Modbus

This section shows the status and the error conditions of the PC Modbus master and slave connections to the controller. For more information about PC Modbus connections, refer to PC Modbus Configuration.

Fieldbus Device

This section shows the status and the error conditions of the Hilscher fieldbus device connections to the controller. For more information about Fieldbus connections, refer to Fieldbus Options Overview.

Communication Service

This section shows that status of the Communication Service connections.
