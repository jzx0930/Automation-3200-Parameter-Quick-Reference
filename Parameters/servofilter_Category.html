Filters Parameter Category

Use the parameters in this category to configure the user-programmable digital filters that are available in the servo loop. Most A3200 drives have eight digital filters in the servo loop. The Ndrive FLS and Nmark CLS have four digital filters in the servo loop. The GL4, the XC2, the XC4, the XC4e, the XR3, and the Nmark GCL have sixteen digital filters in the servo loop.
