IFOV: Configuring the Encoder Feedback

The procedure for configuring encoder feedback is different for each drive type and the options on each drive. Select the procedure that applies to your drive type. The procedures and examples in each topic show you how to use the IFOV AXISPAIR and ENCODER OUT ON Commands in your IFOV program based on the type of drive you are using.
