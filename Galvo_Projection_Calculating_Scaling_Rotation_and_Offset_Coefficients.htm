Calculating Scaling, Rotation, and Offset Coefficients in the Same Operation

NOTE: If you applied offset and rotation corrections, only two points are necessary to calculate the scaling coefficients. Refer to Calculating Scaling Coefficients for more information.

You can calculate scaling, rotation, and offset coefficients in the same operation. To calculate these coefficients, you must measure three points on the part (X1,Y1), (X2,Y2), and (X3,Y3). The three measured points and three commanded points make a linear system that has six equations and six unknown coefficients. As a result, you must solve the linear system to calculate the unknown coefficients.

NOTE: All values for Measured and Command positions are in encoder counts.
