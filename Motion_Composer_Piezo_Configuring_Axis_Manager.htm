Configuring the Motion Composer Display

By default, the Motion Composer display shows position feedback (ProgramPositionFeedback) and velocity feedback (ProgramVelocityFeedback).

Aerotech recommends that you use piezo voltage (PiezoVoltageCommand) instead of velocity feedback (ProgramVelocityFeedback) for your piezo stage. You can use piezo voltage and position feedback to help configure your piezo stage and in applications where you must change between open-loop mode (the SERVO OFF command) and closed-loop mode (the SERVO ON command).

To show piezo voltage in the Motion Composer display

  1. Select Tools → Preferences → AxisManager.
  2. In the Display section of the Preferences dialog, change Secondary Display Type to PiezoVoltageCommand.
Figure: Before You Configure the Motion Composer Display

The AxisManager now shows position feedback and the voltage command for the piezo stage.

Figure: After You Configure the Motion Composer Display
