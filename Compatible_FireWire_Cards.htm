Compatible FireWire or HyperWire™ Cards

Your can configure the A3200 to operate with drives that use either FireWire or HyperWire.

FireWire Cards

Aerotech supports many of the commercially-available FireWire cards and FireWire chipsets that are integrated onto motherboards. The following table shows the types of FireWire cards with chipsets that you can use.

Note: Aerotech does not support laptop PCs and PCMCIA-based FireWire cards.

NOTE: Do not install the FireWire or HyperWire card in a PCI Express or PCI expansion chassis. You must install the card in a PCI Express or PCI slot, Í4 or larger, on the motherboard.

(1) The TI XIO2200 FireWire chipset is not compatible with some motherboard chipsets. Aerotech recommends that you do not install this card on new systems.

(2) A 1394A to 1394B adapter cable is required.

If more than one FireWire card is installed, or if you have a FireWire card and a HyperWire card installed, specify the FireWire card to use for the A3200. Refer to FireWire or HyperWire™ Card Options for information about how to select a card.

HyperWire™ Cards

Aerotech supports all available HyperWire cards.

If more than one HyperWire card is installed, or if you have a FireWire card and a HyperWire card installed, specify the HyperWire card to use for the A3200. Refer to FireWire or HyperWire™ Card Options for information about how to select a card.
