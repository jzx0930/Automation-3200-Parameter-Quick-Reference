Mathematical and Trigonometric Functions

Syntax:

 
<return> = FUNCTION(<Argument> [,<Argument>])

Example:

 
$Var = SQRT(6.7)
  $Var = LOG(64,2)

The table that follows shows the functions, their results, and all restrictions on their input arguments. Restricted operations, such as the square root of a negative number, cause a compiler error or runtime error.

Table:     Mathematical Functions

Function Description of Result Syntax Example

ABS(a)

Absolute value of a

 
ABS(-1.25)      ' 1.25

FRAC(a)

Fractional part (removes integer part) of a (preserves sign)

 
FRAC(-1.25)     ' -0.25

INT(a)

Integer value (truncation of fraction) of a

 
INT(-1.25)      ' -1

EXP(a)

e raised to the power of a

 
EXP(2)   ' 7.389056

LOG(a, b)

Logarithm of a, in base b. a > 0

 
LOG(16,2)       ' 4

SQRT(a)

Square root of a, a ≥ 0

 
SQRT(64)        ' 8

RAND(a)

Pseudorandom value in the range of 0.0 to 1.0

 
RAND(0)  ' Random Value

FLOOR(a)

The largest integral value not larger than a

 
FLOOR(-1.3)     ' -2

CEIL(a)

The smallest integral value not less than a

 
CEIL(8.3)       ' 9

ROUND(a)

The integral value nearest to a

 
ROUND(12.4)     ' 12

INDEXTOMASK(a)

The mask representation of the index a

 
INDEXTOMASK(3)  ' 8

MASKTOINDEX(a)

The index representation of the mask a

 
MASKTOINDEX(16) ' 4

The table that follows shows the trigonometric functions, their restrictions on inputs, and a description of their results.

Table:     Trigonometric Functions

Function Restrictions Description of Result

ACOS (a)

-1 ≤ a ≤ 1

Trigonometric inverse cosine of a (result in radians, 0 to p)

ASIN (a)

-1 ≤ a ≤ 1

Trigonometric inverse sine of a (result in radians, -p/2 to +p/2)

ATAN2 (a,b)

 

Trigonometric inverse tangent of a/b (result in radians, -p to +p)

ATAN (a)

 

Trigonometric inverse tangent of a (result in radians, -p/2 to +p/2)

COS (a)

 

Trigonometric cosine of a (a in radians, result is from 1.0 to -1.0)

SIN (a)

 

Trigonometric sine of a (a in radians, result is from 1.0 to -1.0)

TAN (a)

 

Trigonometric tangent of a (a in radians)
