Galvo Program Structure

Use the following general structure in your program for your GL4 or Nmark CLS, GCL, or SSaM.

1. Configure the laser

The GL4 and the Nmark CLS, GCL, and SSaM each have three laser output signals. Use the Galvo Laser Configuration commands to specify the behavior of these signals. The GL4 and the Nmark drives support five different laser output modes. Each mode has adjustable timing that is specified by additional commands.

WARNING: Refer to your laser manual and obey all of the safety precautions for the laser. To prevent injury and equipment damage, make sure that the laser output signals are configured correctly before you do an operation that causes the laser to fire.

2. Configure delays

Use the configurable Laser Delays to prevent uneven marking at the start or end of a move.

3. Do motion

Your GL4 or Nmark CLS, GCL, or SSaM is now ready for Scanning and Marking. Your program can safely do these operations.

4. Optimize laser power

If you want the controller to change the power of the laser based on the position of the two galvo axes, use Galvo Power Correction.
