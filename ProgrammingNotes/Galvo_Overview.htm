Galvo Overview

The GL4 is a Galvo Linear Drive. The Nmark series of drives includes the CLS (Closed Loop Scanner Control), GCL (Galvo Compact Linear), and SSaM (Synchronized Scanner and Motion). The GL4 and the Nmark drives are galvo scanner control modules that synchronize galvo and servo motion, expand the scanner's field of view, and broaden its application base while maintaining the high throughput capabilities expected from scanners.

Warning: The GL4 and the Nmark CLS, GCL, and SSaM control laser scan systems. When you install and operate the GL4 or the Nmark drive, you must obey all of the safety precautions for a laser. You are responsible for the laser safety of the entire system.

Hardware Installation and Configuration

The hardware manuals supply information about the installation, configuration, electrical connections, and specifications of your drive. The manuals are on your software installation device and are installed with the software. If you accepted the default location when you installed the software, the manuals are in C:\Program Files\Aerotech\A3200\Manuals. You can also download your manual from the Manuals, Help Files, & Cable Drawings section of www.aerotech.com.

To see a list of available hardware manuals, refer to Other Documentation.

Configuring Calibration for the GL4 or the Nmark CLS or GCL

Use one of these procedures to calibrate your GL4 or Nmark CLS or GCL.

  • In Configuration Manager, in the Calibration folder, specify the 2D Galvo Calibration File that you want to use for the GL4 or the Nmark CLS or GCL.
  • In your program, use the LOADCALFILE Command to load the calibration. For more information about loading calibration files from your program, refer to Galvo Program Structure.

Configuring Calibration for the Nmark SSaM

If you have an image field correction file from your scanner manufacturer (a Scanlab CTB or Raylase GCD file), you must use the Galvo Calibration utility to convert the file into GCAL format. For more information, refer to Help in Galvo Calibration File Converter. You can download Galvo Calibration File Converter at www.aerotech.com.

After you have a GCAL file, use one of these procedures to calibrate your Nmark SSaM.

  • In Configuration Manager, in the GCal Files (Nmark SSaM) folder under the Calibration folder, specify the Galvo GCal Calibration data for each axis of the Nmark SSaM.
  • In your program, use the LOADCALFILE Command to load the calibration. For more information about loading calibration files from your program, refer to Galvo Program Structure.
